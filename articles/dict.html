Phuc

noun /f-uu-cke/

A large group of asian people (mainly chinese and vietnamese)
That rice field is run by phuc

Bich

noun /b-ii-ch/

People who eat an excessive amount of rice
My classmate ChingChong is a bich.

Chinker

noun /ch-inn-k-err/

People who get rich from running rice fields and selling rice patties
My classmate Ching Chong's dad is a chinker
