Roller Coaster Collapses in Downtown Miami

On Tuesday at slip and fun water park an roller coaster
reportedly crashed at 3:35pm. Officials say the roller
coaster crash was caused by a loose screw. The park's president
made the following statement. "We are very sorry for the families
that were affected by this event. In the future we will be moving
